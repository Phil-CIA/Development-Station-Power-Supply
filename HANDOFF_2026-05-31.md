## Known Pitfall and Recovery
PlatformIO package/install race can occur if multiple runs overlap.
Symptoms include missing framework files or locked firmware artifacts.

Recovery:
1. Stop stale platformio/python processes.
2. Re-run build/upload sequentially (one command at a time).
3. If framework files are missing, recover from .platformio/.cache/tmp/pkg-installing-*.
